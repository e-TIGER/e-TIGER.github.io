HOME



e-TIGER LLC is developing our namesake mobile app - bringing 21st century technology to the world of politics and empowering our users to get heard - and get heard now.


The first of it's kind and free to our users, we identify, track, communicate and account your opinion to congress at both the state and federal level. By simplifying every aspect of this process to a few clicks with your thumb, we promote transparency, accountability and engagement.


We Empower Democracy.

About



Hello!
Thank you for visiting our webpage. We are a pre-seed company founded on November 21, 2016 in Cincinnati, Ohio.


Founded by Daniel Widrich and Sean Cook, two developers who met through freeCodeCamp.com's software engineering bootcamp, Dan and Sean are both passionate about using technology to forward society and leave the world a better place than they found it.


Our current mission is to Empower Democracy through our namesake mobile app, e-TIGER. Please submit your email address for information and updates on our progress, releases, beta testing, and kickstarter campaign.
